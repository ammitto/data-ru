Everyone can freely travel outside the Russian Federation. A citizen of the Russian Federation has the right to return to the Russian Federation without hindrance.



Chapter I. General provisions



Article 1. Departure from the Russian Federation and entry into the Russian Federation (including transit through its territory) are regulated by the Constitution of the Russian Federation, international treaties of the Russian Federation, this Federal Law, other federal laws, as well as decrees of the President of the Russian Federation adopted on the basis of these federal laws, resolutions of the Government of the Russian Federation. (As amended by federal laws of 10.01.2003 № 7-FZ; dated 13.05.2008 № 65-FZ)

In the event that an international treaty of the Russian Federation establishes rules other than those provided for by this Federal Law, the rules of the international treaty shall apply.

Decisions of interstate bodies adopted on the basis of the provisions of international treaties of the Russian Federation in their interpretation contrary to the Constitution of the Russian Federation are not subject to execution in the Russian Federation. Such a contradiction can be established in the manner determined by the federal constitutional law. (Addition in part - Federal Law of 08.12.2020 № 429-FZ)



Article 2. A citizen of the Russian Federation may not be restricted in his right to leave the Russian Federation except in accordance with this Federal Law. (As amended by the Federal Law of 04.08.2023 № 432-FZ)

A citizen of the Russian Federation cannot be deprived of the right to enter the Russian Federation.

The departure of a citizen of the Russian Federation from the Russian Federation does not entail any restrictions on him or close relatives on the rights guaranteed by the legislation of the Russian Federation and international obligations of the Russian Federation. (As amended by the Federal Law of 01.07.2021 № 270-FZ)

For the purposes of this Federal Law, close relatives are understood as a spouse, parents (adoptive parents), children (adoptive parents), spouses of children, full and half-blooded brothers and sisters, grandparents, grandmothers, grandchildren. (Addition by part - Federal Law of 01.07.2021 № 270-FZ)



Article 3. The procedure for crossing the State Border of the Russian Federation when leaving the Russian Federation and entering the Russian Federation is regulated by the Law of the Russian Federation "On the State Border of the Russian Federation" and this Federal Law.



Article 4. Citizens of the Russian Federation who are outside the Russian Federation are under the protection and patronage of the Russian Federation.

Diplomatic missions and consular institutions of the Russian Federation are obliged to provide measures to protect citizens of the Russian Federation and provide them with patronage in the manner determined by the legislation of the Russian Federation and international treaties of the Russian Federation.



Article 5. In the event of an emergency situation on the territory of a foreign state, the Russian Federation guarantees the adoption of diplomatic, economic and other measures provided for by international law to ensure the security of citizens of the Russian Federation staying on the territory of this foreign state.

In the event that circumstances arise on the territory of any foreign state that seriously hinder the adoption of measures to protect citizens of the Russian Federation and the provision of patronage to them by the Russian Federation, the Government of the Russian Federation shall take measures to bring to the attention of citizens of the Russian Federation recommendations on the undesirability of entry into this state. Such recommendations are not grounds for a temporary restriction of the right to leave the Russian Federation.



Article 6. Citizens of the Russian Federation shall exit from the Russian Federation and enter the Russian Federation on the basis of valid identity documents of a citizen of the Russian Federation outside the territory of the Russian Federation, and in cases established by international treaties of the Russian Federation, on the basis of a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation certifying the identity of a citizen of the Russian Federation on the territory of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as an internal Russian passport). The procedure for registration, issuance and withdrawal of such documents is determined by this Federal Law. The procedure established by Chapter II of this Federal Law, except for the case established by part one of Article 11 of this Federal Law, and the procedure established by Article 18 of this Federal Law, shall not apply to the internal Russian passport. (As amended by federal laws of 13.06.2023 № 212-FZ, dated 23.07.2025 № 257-FZ)

Foreign citizens or stateless persons are obliged to present valid documents proving their identity and recognized by the Russian Federation in this capacity when entering the Russian Federation and leaving the Russian Federation, and a visa, unless otherwise provided by international treaties of the Russian Federation, this Federal Law or decrees of the President of the Russian Federation. (As amended by federal laws of 24.06.1999 № 118-FZ; of 10.01.2003 № 7-FZ; of 01.12.2007 № 310-FZ; of 13.05.2008 № 65-FZ; of 07.06.2017 № 111-FZ)

Stateless persons shall enter and leave the Russian Federation in accordance with the rules established by this Federal Law for foreign citizens, unless otherwise provided by international treaties of the Russian Federation, this Federal Law, other federal laws or decrees of the President of the Russian Federation. (Addition in part - Federal Law of 24.06.1999 № 118-FZ) (As amended by Federal Laws of 10.01.2003 № 7-FZ; of 13.05.2008 № 65-FZ; of 07.06.2017 № 111-FZ)

The formation of information about foreign citizens and stateless persons in the information systems of state bodies and organizations is ensured by uniform translation of surnames and names contained in identity documents of foreign citizens and stateless persons from Latin to Cyrillic. The procedure for the specified translation of surnames and names, including for the purpose of conducting experiments provided for in Article 2516-2 of this Federal Law, shall be established by the Government of the Russian Federation. (Addition by part - Federal Law of 08.08.2024 № 253-FZ)

Control over the availability of visas or other permits for the entry of persons into a foreign state is the responsibility of the transport company (passenger carrier), unless otherwise provided by an international treaty of the Russian Federation.

For the purposes of this Federal Law, the following shall not be considered as an exit from the Russian Federation and as an entry into the Russian Federation:

crossing of the State Border of the Russian Federation by a citizen of the Russian Federation, a foreign citizen or a stateless person during the validity of his visa when traveling from one part of the territory of the Russian Federation to another part of its territory through the territory of a foreign state in transit transit or through the exclusive economic zone of the Russian Federation and exclusive economic zones of foreign states, through the open seas, and in cases established by the Government of the Russian Federation, through the territorial seas of foreign states without entering foreign ports, as well as when following Russian aircraft, which in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation can repeatedly cross the State Border of the Russian Federation without passing border, customs (in terms of customs operations related to the arrival (departure) of vessels) and other types of control;

crossing the State Border of the Russian Federation by a citizen of the Russian Federation, a foreign citizen or a stateless person who are crew members and included in the ship's role of ships, on Russian and foreign vessels, which, in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation, can repeatedly cross the State Border of the Russian Federation without passing border, customs and other types of control. (As amended by the Federal Law of 29.07.2018 № 270-FZ)

Article 81. The grounds for invalidity of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport are:

1) loss of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport, confirmed by a citizen's statement of loss of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport or application by an organization that sent a citizen of the Russian Federation on a business trip outside the territory of the Russian Federation, for loss of a diplomatic passport or service passport, adopted by a federal executive body in the field of internal affairs or its territorial body, federal executive body in charge of foreign affairs, or its territorial body, diplomatic mission or consular institution of the Russian Federation or other federal executive body or federal state body specified in part seven of Article 8 of this Federal Law (hereinafter referred to as the state body issuing a passport, diplomatic passport, official passports);

2) change by the owner of the passport, diplomatic passport or service passport in accordance with the established procedure, surname, patronymic, information about the date (day, month, year) and (or) place of birth, change of gender;

3) expiration of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport;

4) death of the holder of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport;

5) non-return without a valid reason of a diplomatic passport or service passport by a citizen of the Russian Federation within five working days after the completion of the business trip, confirmed by the state body that issued the diplomatic passport, service passport, the message of the organization that sent the citizen of the Russian Federation on a business trip outside the territory of the Russian Federation, which such an organization is obliged to send to this state body within five working days from the date of expiration of the specified period;

6) termination of citizenship of the Russian Federation of the holder of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport, confirmed by a decision to terminate the citizenship of the Russian Federation, adopted by the authorized body in charge of cases of citizenship of the Russian Federation;

7) cancellation of the decision to acquire the owner of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport of the citizenship of the Russian Federation;

8) establishing, based on the results of the inspection carried out within the framework of determining the citizenship of the Russian Federation in accordance with the procedure established by the President of the Russian Federation, that the holder does not have a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport of the citizenship of the Russian Federation;

9) establishing, based on the results of the inspection provided for in part one of Article 82 of this Federal Law, the fact of registration of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport on the basis of forged (false) or invalid documents or inaccurate information or on a false form;

10) establishment of the fact of inaccurate information in the passport, diplomatic passport or service passport by the state body issuing the passport, diplomatic passport, service, or the border body of the Federal Security Service in the manner determined by the Government of the Russian Federation. This procedure should include criteria indicating the presence of inaccurate information in the passport, diplomatic passport, service passport;

11) establishment by the state body issuing a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, or by the border body of the Federal Security Service of the fact that a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport is unsuitable for further use in the manner determined by the Government of the Russian Federation. This procedure should include signs of unsuitability of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport for further use;

12) non-receipt of the passport by its owner after three years from the date of passport issuance;

13) non-receipt of a diplomatic passport or service passport by an organization sending a citizen of the Russian Federation on a business trip outside the territory of the Russian Federation, after one year from the date of issuance of a diplomatic passport or service passport;

14) failure to transfer a passport without a valid reason by a citizen of the Russian Federation, whose right to leave the Russian Federation is restricted on the basis provided for in subparagraph 1 of Article 15 of this Federal Law, for storage in an authorized body or organization in accordance with part one of Article 18 of this Federal Law, confirmed by the state body that issued the passport, by a message from the authorized body or organization, which the authorized body or organization are obliged to send within five working days from the date of expiration of the period provided for in the said part, to the state body that issued the passport;

15) registration of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport on a lost (stolen) form.

Passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are invalid:

1) on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 3, 4, 12 and 13 of the first part of this article, from the date of occurrence of the relevant circumstances;

2) on the basis provided for in subparagraph 2 of part one of this article, after six months after the date of occurrence of the relevant circumstances;

3) on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 6 and 7 of part one of this article, from the date of adoption of the relevant decision by the authorized body in charge of cases of citizenship of the Russian Federation;

4) on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 8, 9 and 15 of the first part of this article, from the date of issuance of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport;

5) on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 10 and 11 of the first part of this article, from the date of adoption of the relevant decision by the state body issuing a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, or the border body of the Federal Security Service;

6) on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 1, 5 and 14 of the first part of this article, from the date of acceptance of the relevant application or petition or receipt of the relevant communication.

Information on the invalidity of the passport in the cases specified in subparagraphs 1 - 12 and 14 of the first part of this article shall be subject to the accounting provided for in part three of Article 91 of this Federal Law, except in the case of establishing the fact of its registration on a false form.

Passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are subject to withdrawal by the state body issuing a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, and referral to the state body that issued the passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, in cases where:

1) signs of invalidity of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport were found on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 9 - 11 of the first part of this article;

2) passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are invalid on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 5 - 8, 14 and 15 of part one of this article;

3) passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are presented by a person who is not the owner of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport.

Passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are subject to withdrawal by the border body of the Federal Security Service and referral to the state body that issued the passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, in cases where:

1) passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are invalid on the grounds provided for in subparagraphs 5 - 11, 14 and 15 of the first part of this article;

2) passport, diplomatic passport, service passport are presented by a person who is not the owner of the passport, diplomatic passport, service passport.

In case of withdrawal of a passport, diplomatic passport or service passport, a document on its withdrawal is drawn up. Such a withdrawal can be appealed in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation.

The procedure and terms of seizure and sending of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport to the state body that issued the passport, diplomatic passport, service passport, in the cases provided for in parts four and five of this article, the procedure for drawing up a document on the withdrawal of a passport, diplomatic passport, service passport shall be established by the Government of the Russian Federation.

(Supplement by the article - Federal Law of 13.06.2023 № 212-FZ)

Article 9. To apply for a passport, a citizen of the Russian Federation must indicate his surname, first name, patronymic (including previously existing ones), gender, date and place of birth, place of residence, place of work (service, study) for the last ten years and submit the main document certifying his identity in a written application for a passport of the established sample. Article 15. The right of a citizen of the Russian Federation to leave the Russian Federation may be temporarily restricted in cases where he:

1) had access to information of special importance or top secret information.

The decision to restrict the right to leave the Russian Federation of judges of federal courts of general jurisdiction and federal arbitration courts who are aware of special importance or top secret information is made in accordance with the provision approved by the Judicial Department under the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation in coordination with the Presidium of the Council of Judges of the Russian Federation.

The decision to restrict the right to leave the Russian Federation of other citizens who are aware of information of special importance or top secret information shall be made by officials vested with appropriate powers, in the manner determined by the Government of the Russian Federation, unless otherwise established by the President of the Russian Federation.

If there is a conclusion of the Interdepartmental Commission for the Protection of State Secrets that information of special importance or top-secret information in which the citizen was aware retains an appropriate degree of secrecy, the period of restriction of the right to leave the Russian Federation may be extended by the interdepartmental commission formed in accordance with the procedure established for the establishment of interdepartmental coordination and advisory bodies formed by federal executive bodies. Chapter VI. The procedure for transit of foreign citizens and stateless persons through the territory of the Russian Federation



Article 29. Transit through the territory of the Russian Federation is usually carried out without the right to stop.

Unless otherwise provided by a federal law or an international treaty of the Russian Federation, transit through the territory of the Russian Federation of foreign citizens and stateless persons to the state of destination by all modes of transport is allowed upon presentation of a Russian transit visa, a visa to enter the state adjacent to the Russian Federation along the route or a visa of the state of destination and travel tickets valid for leaving the Russian Federation or a confirmed guarantee of their purchase at the transfer point on the territory of the Russian Federation. (As amended by federal laws of 24.06.1999 № 118-FZ; of 06.12.2011 № 400-FZ)

A transit visa is not required for a foreign citizen in case of transit passage of this foreign citizen through the territory of the Russian Federation in non-stop mode on sections of transport highways in accordance with the list and in the manner established by the Government of the Russian Federation. (Addition by part - Federal Law of 10.01.2003 № 7-FZ)

Transit travel of a foreign citizen or a stateless person in accordance with the international treaty of the Russian Federation on readmission is carried out on the basis of a decision on the transit of the specified foreign citizen or stateless person, adopted by the federal executive body in the field of internal affairs or its territorial body. (Addition in part - Federal Law of 06.12.2011 № 400-FZ) (As amended by the Federal Law of 01.07.2017 № 131-FZ)

The list of officials of the federal executive body in the field of internal affairs and its territorial bodies authorized to make a decision on transit, specified in part four of this article, shall be approved by the federal executive body in the field of internal affairs. (Addition part - Federal Law of 01.07.2017 № 131-FZ)



Article 30. A visa with the right to stop on the territory of the Russian Federation may be issued to foreign citizens or stateless persons upon presentation of a reasoned request and documents confirming the need for a stop, unless otherwise provided by an international treaty of the Russian Federation.

The request specified in part one of this article and documents confirming the need for a stop may be submitted in the form of electronic documents. (Addition in part - Federal Law of 27.07.2010 № 227-FZ)



Article 31. Transit through the territory of the Russian Federation without a visa is allowed to a foreign citizen or a stateless person in cases where they:

1) make a non-stop flight by air through the territory of the Russian Federation;

2) follow an international airline with a transfer at the airport on the territory of the Russian Federation and have duly executed documents for the right to enter the state of destination and a ticket with a confirmed date of departure from the transfer airport on the territory of the Russian Federation within 24 hours from the moment of arrival, except in cases of forced stop;

3) reside in the territory of the state with which the Russian Federation has a relevant international treaty.



Article 32. A forced stop is a stay of more than 24 hours within the settlement due to the occurrence of the following circumstances:

1) natural disasters that delay the movement of a train, vehicle, ship or aircraft;

2) the need to repair the relevant vehicle damaged as a result of damage to any of its parts or as a result of a traffic accident;

3) diseases, if, according to the doctor's conclusion, further follow-up of the patient seems dangerous to his life and health;

4) unforeseen delays when transferring from one mode of transport to another at the transfer point.

In case of forced stop, registration of stay on the territory of the Russian Federation and extension of the validity of the transit Russian visa are carried out by the territorial body of the federal executive body in the field of internal affairs at the place of forced stop at the request of foreign citizens or stateless persons. (As amended by federal laws of 18.07.2006 № 121-FZ; of 01.07.2017 № 131-FZ)
